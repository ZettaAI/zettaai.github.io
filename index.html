About Zetta

We are a research group that is advancing the state-of-the-art in neuronal wiring diagram reconstruction, while offering automatic reconstruction services to neuroscience labs.

Our team created the automatic reconstructions behind two groundbreaking achievements in neuroscience:

  • MICrONS: The MICrONS consortium achieved the first reconstruction of a cubic millimeter of the cerebral cortex, demonstrating petascale connectomics with our AI.
  • FlyWire: Using our AI, the FlyWire project mapped the adult Drosophila brain with over 100,000 neurons and 100 million synapses, a massive leap beyond the 300 neurons of C. elegans.

We are key contributors to BRAIN CONNECTS, a ten-year effort under the US BRAIN Initiative to map a whole mouse brain at synaptic resolution and reach exascale connectomics.

A human brain imaged at synaptic resolution would yield a zettabyte of data (1e21 bytes). Our ultimate goal is to enable zettascale connectomics.

Reconstruction services

We currently offer:

  • Image stitching
  • Image alignment
  • Cell segmentation
  • Nucleus, blood vessel, compartment, organelle segmentations
  • Synapse detection & assignment
  • Collaborative proofreading & analysis via CAVE
  • Ad hoc processing
